Spinach Dip

Prep Time: 10 Minutes

Cook Time: N/A

Total Time: 10 Minutes

Servings: Feeds 5-10 people

Description

A great tasting, easy to make spinach dip perfect as an appetizer for any party or get together!

Ingredients

  • Frozen Chopped Spinach 10oz
  • Sour Cream one 16oz container
  • Mayonnaise 1 cup, Hellmann's® or Best Foods® is preferred
  • Knorr® Vegetable recipe mix 1 package
  • Water Chestnuts 1 can 8oz
  • Scallions bulb only
  • Green Onions 3 (optional)

Steps

This recipe is good for a party setting as an appetizer, and can feed 5-10 people.

  1. Thaw the box of frozen chopped spinach using your preferred method. The microwave is a quick and easy way just follow the instructions on the spinach packaging.
  2. Follow the directions on the Knorr® Recipe Mix reduce it to a thicker consistency and leave it sitting to cool.
  3. Drain and chop the 8oz can of chestnuts.
  4. Chop the 3 green onions and the scallion bulbs store the green stem of the scallions for later use.
  5. Once the cooked Knorr® Recipe Mix has cooled, and the chopped spinach has fully thawed, pull out a medium to large sized mixing bowl and mix all the ingredients together well. Using a small mixer on slow speed can help make the process easier, but a wooden spoon or spatula will work just as well.
  6. Once mixed cover the bowl with cling on and leave it to sit in the fridge for a few hours, overnight is better, or until the entire mixture has chilled to a firm consistency.
  7. Transfer the dip into your preferred serving bowl, with some crackers chips or vegetables and enjoy!
