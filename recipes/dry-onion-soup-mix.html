Dry Onion Soup Mix Recipe

This is a easy homemade, gluten and milk free onion soup mix recipe. Very easy and quick to make, perfect for using as an alternative to a store bought soup mix in any French Dip Au Jus recipe!

Prep Time: 5 Minutes

Cook Time: N/A

Total Time: 5 Minutes

Servings: 1 oz

Calories: 20kcal

Author: Brandie Skibinski

Recipe courtesy of The Country Cook.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup dried onion flakes
  • 2 tablespoons sodium-free beef bouillon granules
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ⅛ teaspoon celery seed
  • ⅛ teaspoon paprika
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Instructions

Makes 1oz.

  1. Measure all of the ingredients into a bowl or a lidded container.
  2. Mix Together well. Stir in a lidded container in a cool dry place.
  3. Store for up to 6 months.
